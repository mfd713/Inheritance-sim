Sims 4 Legacy Challenge: Trait Generator

Welcome! This is a tool for randomly generating sims traits, while also allowing for a little "genetic" influence based on the parents' traits.

Use the form below to enter the parents' traits. You can choose a genotype to determine the strength of heritability, or plug in the genotypes of previously-generated parents. Click the button and see a whole new sim come to life!
